Timeline of iPhone Models

2007 - iPhone 1st Gen

The original iPhone changed the smartphone landscape forever.

2007 - iPhone 1st Gen

The original iPhone changed the smartphone landscape forever.

2008 - iPhone 3G

Introduced 3G connectivity and the App Store, revolutionizing the app ecosystem.

2009 - iPhone 3GS

Improved speed and introduced video recording capabilities.

2010 - iPhone 4

Launched with a Retina Display and a new flat design.

2011 - iPhone 4S

Introduced Siri, the voice-activated personal assistant.

2012 - iPhone 5

Featured a larger screen and LTE connectivity.

2013 - iPhone 5S & 5C

First to include Touch ID; the 5C offered a colorful, affordable option.

2014 - iPhone 6 & 6 Plus

Introduced larger screens and Apple Pay.

2016 - iPhone SE

A compact and affordable iPhone with the performance of the 6S.

2017 - iPhone X

Celebrated the 10th anniversary with Face ID and an all-screen design.

2018 - iPhone XS & XR

Refined the Face ID design and introduced an affordable XR model.

2019 - iPhone 11 Series

Enhanced cameras and battery life, with the Pro models introducing triple cameras.

2020 - iPhone 12 Series

First with 5G connectivity and a new flat-edge design.

2021 - iPhone 13 Series

Incremental upgrades with a smaller notch and improved battery life.

2022 - iPhone 14 Series

Introduced the Dynamic Island feature and advanced safety features.
